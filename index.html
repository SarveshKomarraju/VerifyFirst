Why BEC Matters

One deceptive email can cause significant financial loss.

Business Email Compromise is among the most financially damaging forms of cybercrime because it exploits trust rather than technical vulnerabilities. Instead of deploying malware, attackers impersonate executives, vendors, or trusted partners to convince victims to transfer money or disclose sensitive information.

Organizations of every size are vulnerable because the attacks often appear legitimate and rely on psychology, urgency, and familiarity rather than sophisticated exploits.

About VerifyFirst

An independent research initiative focused on strengthening email security.

VerifyFirst was developed to explore one of today's most significant cybersecurity challenges: Business Email Compromise. The project combines independent academic research with educational resources designed to help readers understand how these attacks succeed and how they can be prevented.

Rather than focusing solely on technical controls, VerifyFirst emphasizes awareness, critical thinking, and evidence-based security education. The project reflects the belief that informed users are one of the strongest defenses against social engineering attacks.
