# VerifyFirst

## Independent Business Email Compromise (BEC) Cybersecurity Research Initiative

VerifyFirst is an independent cybersecurity research project focused on understanding, analyzing, and educating others about **Business Email Compromise (BEC)** attacks.

The project combines original research, cybersecurity education, interactive simulations, and threat analysis tools to help users understand how attackers exploit trust, social engineering, and email communication systems.

---

# Project Overview

Business Email Compromise is one of the most financially damaging forms of cybercrime. Unlike traditional malware-based attacks, BEC relies heavily on impersonation, deception, and human decision-making.

VerifyFirst was created to explore:

- How BEC attacks operate
- Common attacker techniques
- Warning signs that indicate suspicious communication
- Defensive strategies organizations can implement
- The importance of security awareness and verification procedures

# Features

VerifyFirst includes an independent research publication analyzing Business Email Compromise through:

## Threat Intelligence Dashboard

An interactive threat intelligence environment that allows users to explore:

- BEC attack scenarios
- Attacker techniques
- Investigation findings
- Indicators of compromise
- Defensive recommendations

---

## BEC Attack Lab

A simulated investigation environment where users analyze reconstructed Business Email Compromise campaigns.

Features include:

- Attack scenario analysis
- MITRE ATT&CK technique mapping
- Evidence review
- Investigation workflow
- Analyst findings

---

## Case Studies

Interactive case studies examining real-world BEC incidents.

Users can explore:

- Attack methods
- Financial impact
- Attacker behavior
- Security lessons learned

---

## Practice Simulator

A training environment designed to help users recognize suspicious emails.

Users analyze:

- Email content
- Sender information
- Social engineering indicators
- Recommended security responses
